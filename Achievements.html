Accomplishments and Recognition

Richard Turner has won the Close-Up Magician of the Year from The Academy of Magical Arts (AMA).
The award was presented in Los Angeles on May 17, 2015.
The AMA promotes the art of magic, encourages fellowship, and maintains the highest ethical standards.
Their annual awards show is devoted to the promotion and development of magic, and the magicians who practice the craft.
The awards event has taken place since the 1960s.

Richard Turner has ,also, won the Penn and Teller:Fool Us show
(the biggest TV show about magic in the world), managing to fool both Penn and Teller with his card tricks.
The two magicians didn't even get to the point of trying to understand what the secret was
behind Turner's tricks. At this show Penn and Teller -two great magicians of their own- invite some of the world's best magicians
and ask them to perform a trick for them. After the called-up magician performs his trick,
the two magicians try to understand how he did it.
If wrong, the magician wins the opportunity to later perform at one of Penn and Teller's Las Vegas shows.

The Dealt movie is an autobiography movie about Richard Turner, in which Richard plays himself.
The movie has won 4 awards while it has been nominated for another 2.
It has won the audience award at Dallas Internation Film Festival (2017), while it was ,also, nominated for the Grand Jury Prize.
The movie has won the Best Documentary award at dead CENTER film festival (2017) and ,again, the audience
award at the Independent Film Festival of Boston(2017). Finally, Turner's movie has won the audience award at the SXSW Film Festival (2017),
while being nominated ,also, for the SXSW Grand Jury Award.
